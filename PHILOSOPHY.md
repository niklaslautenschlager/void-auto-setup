# My Philosophy

I do not like "vibe coding," and I am generally skeptical of AI-generated code and AI in general.

That said, I used AI as a tool while making this project for a practical reason: automation. My goal with this script is to reduce repetitive setup work and make it easier for more people to get into Void Linux.

Void Linux is the only distro that really feels like home to me. I care a lot about it, and one of my personal goals is to make it more approachable and user-friendly, especially for people who want a smoother starting point.

I am also still early in my computer science studies. If there are bugs, rough edges, or places where the implementation shows a lack of knowledge, that is mostly due to my current level of experience rather than a lack of care.

This project is a learning process, a convenience tool, and a small effort to help more people enjoy Void Linux.
